Body Scan Meditation

Gently bring awareness to each part of your body to release tension and stress.

Find a comfortable position, either sitting or lying down.

Close your eyes and take 3 deep breaths.

Starting from your toes, gradually move your attention up through your body.

Notice any sensations but don't judge or try to change them.

Breathe into areas of tension, imagining them softening.
